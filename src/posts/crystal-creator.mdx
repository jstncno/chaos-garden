<BlogCard date={frontmatter.publishDate} links={frontmatter.links}>
  **CrystalCreator** is an interactive web application based off of Matthew
  Epler's YouTube series on
  [Designing Generative Systems with p5.js][gensystemsvid]
  (with an extra shout out to Stephan Max for
  [more inspiration][genartbloglink]). It creates geometric
  `"crystals"` from a set of randomly generated parameters.

  It was initially created in 2020 to help combat shelter-in-place boredom and
  depression. It served as a creative outlet and marks the beginning of my
  discovery and exploration of generative art.

  Interact with the full application [here][crystalcreator].
</BlogCard>
